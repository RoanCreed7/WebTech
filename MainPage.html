Infiltration Adventure Game
MISSION BRIEF:
You are an agent of a small government body with the mission of setting yourself up to be a spy inside an opposing powerful nation. This nation has personal data and surveillance on every person in the known world, including you. The only way to safely live there is by deleting your data file from their master server hidden in the mountains. You must infiltrate your way through by hacking their defence systems. Good luck.
