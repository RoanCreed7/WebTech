Game Instructions:
As you move through the mission, you will be asked to choose between two options, one of which will provide a time bonus to complete the mini game. Then you must complete the game within the given amount of time or else you are caught and the mission is failed.


Word Scramble:
You will be given a string of letters that have been scrambled from a full word, your job is to unscramble them in the time given. Each word is related to computing. As the difficulty level inceases so does the length of the word(Easy 3/4, Medium 5/6, Hard 7/8).
